About Holmes and Arthur Conan Doyle

Sherlock Holmes is a fictional character from books written by British writer: Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. Originally a physician, Conan Doyle's focus changed when he decided to become a writer in 1886. His first novel, A Study In Scarlet, was published one year later. After seeing positive reviews, and surprising popularity, he went on to write 3 more novels and over fifty short stories.

The stories follow Holmes, a detective who uses logic and deductive reasoning to help solve crimes, and Watson, a doctor who often acts as an assistant and friend to Holmes.
